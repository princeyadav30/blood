Terms and Conditions

Please read these terms and conditions carefully before using our blood donation services.

1. Eligibility

To donate blood, you must meet the following criteria:

  • Be between 18 and 65 years old.
  • Weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • Be in good health and free from infectious diseases.
  • Not have donated blood in the last 56 days.

2. Donation Process

The blood donation process includes the following steps:

  1. Registration and health screening.
  2. Blood donation (takes 10-15 minutes).
  3. Post-donation refreshments and rest.

3. Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and ensure that all personal information is handled securely. Your data will only be used for blood donation purposes.

4. Liability

We are not liable for any health issues arising from blood donation if you do not meet the eligibility criteria or fail to disclose relevant health information.

5. Changes to Terms

We reserve the right to update these terms and conditions at any time. Please review this page periodically for changes.
