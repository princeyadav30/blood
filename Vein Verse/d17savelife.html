Facts About Blood Donation

Knowledge is power - understand how your donation makes a difference

Frequent Need

Every 2 seconds someone in the U.S. needs blood, requiring about 36,000 units of red blood cells each day.

Short Shelf Life

Red blood cells must be used within 42 days, while platelets must be used within just 5 days.

Universal Donors

Type O-negative blood can be given to patients of all blood types, making it especially valuable.

Regular Donors

Only 3% of age-eligible people donate blood yearly, yet 25% will need blood in their lifetime.

Quick Recovery

Your body replaces the fluid lost during donation within 24 hours and red cells within 4-6 weeks.

Multiple Uses

One donation can be separated into red cells, plasma, and platelets to help multiple patients.
